The Debate

Some people think Anakin became Darth Vader when he chose to help Palpatine. Others think it happened later when he put on the suit. This page looks at both sides of the debate.

Possible Moments Anakin Became Darth Vader

  1. When he chose Palpatine over Mace Windu
    • He betrayed the Jedi
    • He helped Palpatine survive
    • He made a choice he could not easily undo
  2. When he attacked the Jedi Temple
    • He fully followed Palpatine's orders
    • He turned against the Jedi Order
    • This is where his actions became truly dark
  3. When he put on the Darth Vader suit
    • He lost his old identity
    • He looked like Darth Vader for the first time
    • This is the version most people recognize
